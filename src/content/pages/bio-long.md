Alex Oladele is a senior software engineer at Red Hat, where he works on Ansible Galaxy as part of the Ansible Automation Platform. He is an active member of the Python community and a member of Black Python Devs.

Alex speaks at conferences and universities about code review culture, building with Python, and the practical skills that rarely make it into a curriculum. Past stages include AfroTech, PyOhio, and a guest lecture at Ontario Tech University.

Outside of work he builds open source side projects, from Discord bots to a local-first mood tracking app, and tinkers endlessly with his homelab. He writes about all of it at alexo.dev.
