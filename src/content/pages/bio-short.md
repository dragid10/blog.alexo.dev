Alex Oladele is a senior software engineer at Red Hat, working on Ansible Galaxy as part of the Ansible Automation Platform. He is a member of Black Python Devs and speaks at conferences about code review culture, Python, and practical engineering skills. He is passionate about mentoring and creating safe, inclusive spaces in tech. Outside of work he builds open source side projects and runs a homelab he is very proud of. He writes at alexo.dev.
