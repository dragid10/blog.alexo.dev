Alex Oladele is a senior software engineer at Red Hat, working on Ansible Galaxy as part of the Ansible Automation Platform. He is a member of Black Python Devs and speaks at conferences about code review culture, Python, and practical engineering skills. Outside of work he builds open source side projects and runs a homelab he is very proud of. He writes at alexo.dev.
