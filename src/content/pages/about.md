Hey, I'm **Alex Oladele**. I'm a senior software engineer at Red Hat, working on [Ansible Galaxy](https://galaxy.ansible.com/) as part of the Ansible Automation Platform.

I grew up in Westerville, Ohio and studied software engineering at Miami University. I hated Python until April 2019. Now I speak about it at conferences. I'm a member of [Black Python Devs](https://blackpythondevs.com/) and a moderator of a [Raleigh-based Mastodon server](https://triangletoot.party/).

I [speak](/speaking/) at conferences and universities about code review culture, Python, and practical engineering skills.

Outside of work I build [open source side projects](/projects/) and run a homelab that I'm very proud of. My fiance and I share a love of [fountain pens and stationery](https://alexo-pens.notion.site/Alex-s-Fountain-Pen-Dashboard-268e3af9171080c4a7c2c8aca9b92a2e).
